What's New?

Welcome to the new web site! I have decided to move away from blog format to a more documentation-based approach. The plan is to write 
more detailed technical articles and talk links, and try and keep them up-to-date. 

    *April, 2022*
    
    + New post: :doc:`posts/securing_mapserver`

    *January, 2022*
    
    + New post: :doc:`posts/nuget`

    *September, 2021*

    + Presenting "State of MapServer" at `FOSS4G 2021 <https://2021.foss4g.org/>`_
      Slides online at https://geographika.github.io/mapserver-state-2021/
	
    *August, 2019*

    + Presenting "State of MapServer" at `FOSS4G 2019 <https://2019.foss4g.org>`_
      Slides online at https://geographika.github.io/mapserver-state-2019/
    + Presenting "State of GeoExt" at `FOSS4G 2019 <https://2019.foss4g.org>`_
      Slides online at https://geographika.github.io/geoext-state-2019/

    *April, 2019*

    + `Third Irish OSGeo Symposium 2019 <https://wiki.osgeo.org/wiki/Ireland/Symposium2019>`_
      Lightning talk - `Lightning Mapping using OSGeo's MapCache <http://www.cs.nuim.ie/~pmooney/OSGeoIE2019/5.pdf>`_
..
    *July, 2017*

    + `mappyfile <http://mappyfile.readthedocs.io/en/latest/>`_ 0.3.2 released.
    + Presented mappyfile at `FOSS4G Europe <https://europe.foss4g.org/2017/Home>`_ - slides available :download:`here</files/foss4ge2017_mappyfile_sgirvin.pdf>`.

    + All WordPress posts and comments migrated to this new Sphinx-based web site.
    + `mappyfile <http://mappyfile.readthedocs.io/en/latest/>`_ 0.2.2 released.

    + A talk on *mappyfile* has been accepted for `FOSS4G Europe <https://europe.foss4g.org/2017/Home>`_ in Paris in July.

Who am I?

I'm an independent geodeveloper who designs, creates, and customises Geographic Information Systems (GIS) for the web and desktop. 
Most of my time is spent providing consulting and bespoke geospatial software development to clients in Ireland and Europe.

Get in touch via sethg@geographika.co.uk

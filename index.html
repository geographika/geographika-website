What’s New?

Welcome to the new web site! I have decided to move away from blog format to a more documentation-based approach. The plan is to write more detailed technical articles and talk links, and try and keep them up-to-date.

I’m currently working on a new project MapServer Studio - an online application to work with Mapfiles and dynamically render them with MapServer. Please subscribe by email or follow @mapserverstudio to keep updated on development.
